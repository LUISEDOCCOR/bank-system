about this project

Welcome to "My Flask Project"! This web application, developed with Flask and other technologies such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript, aims to simulate the experience of a bank page. Explore key features, from making transfers and deposits to creating and maintaining users, even allowing you to have your own debit card within the app. Immerse yourself in the complete virtual banking experience with a passionate team behind every detail. We invite you to be part of this exciting online banking simulation!
